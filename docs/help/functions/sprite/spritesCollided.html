spritesCollided(testSpriteName, referenceSpriteName, [ ,contactStruct ])

Description

Returns true if testSpriteName and referenceSpriteName have made contact after the last call to updateSprites(), and false otherwise. Details about the contact are provided by contactStruct, a structure instance containing the following optional fields:

  • time - A value that is greater than or equal to 0, and less than 1, which indicates when the two sprites first made contact within the interval [0,deltaTime). For example, a value of 0.5 indicates that contact was made about half way through the interval [0,deltaTime).
  • normalX - Indicates which sides of testSpriteName and referenceSpriteName have made contact on the X-axis. Has one of the following values:
    • -1 - Indicates that the right side of testSpriteName made contact with the left side of referenceSpriteName.
    • 1 - Indicates that the left side of testSpriteName made contact with the right side of referenceSpriteName.
    • 0 - Indicates no contact on the X-axis.
  • normalY - Indicates which sides of testSpriteName and referenceSpriteName have made contact on the Y-axis. Has one of the following values:
    • -1 - Indicates that the bottom side of testSpriteName made contact with the top side of referenceSpriteName.
    • 1 - Indicates that the top side of testSpriteName made contact with the bottom side of referenceSpriteName.
    • 0 - Indicates no contact on the Y-axis.
